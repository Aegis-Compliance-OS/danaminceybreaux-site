The Platform

One engine.
Every regulatory lens.

Every regulated operation does the work right — and then spends just as long proving it. Aegis turns the records a shop already keeps into a live compliance posture, every requirement scored, so the proof becomes a by-product of the job rather than a separate scramble.

One normalized engine reports against any framework. The same maintenance and operational records answer the FAA, OSHA, EPA, your customers, and an auditor — assess once, report to everyone. Adding a framework is a data load, not a rebuild.

Every piece of evidence is cryptographically sealed the moment it is created, so an auditor can verify it was never altered after the fact — tamper-evident posture, not a binder reconstructed the night before the audit.

Aegis serves regulated maintenance & MRO — aviation, transit, rail, maritime, fleet, and auto-body — alongside infosec frameworks including CMMC, ISO 27001, and SOC 2, all from the same engine.
