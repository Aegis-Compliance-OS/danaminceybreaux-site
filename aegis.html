The Platform

Compliance automation
that lives where your data does.

Aegis deploys as a software license inside the customer's own Microsoft GCC High tenant — inheriting the tenant's existing FedRAMP authorization rather than requiring contractors to trust a third-party cloud. This architectural purity is the core differentiator.

The STARS schema (Structured Technology Assessment & Readiness Schema) maps actual technology configurations to NIST SP 800-171 Rev 2 controls, generates assessor-ready SSP narratives, and enforces non-compliant action blocking in real time — stopping policy violations at the source before they become findings.

Project TRIDENT is the flagship hardware and sensing layer of the Aegis platform — a mixed-reality asset inspection system built on two issued Navy patents licensed from NPS. TRIDENT feeds physical inspection data directly into the STARS schema, extending compliance automation from the digital layer into the physical environment.

Aegis targets FedRAMP 20x authorization via an evidence-based pathway, and expands beyond DIB into aerospace MRO, maritime logistics, critical minerals, medical devices, and fintech.
