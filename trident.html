The Technology

Asset inspection
through mixed reality.

TRIDENT applies two issued Navy patents — US 11,138,805 (Quantitative Quality Assurance for Mixed Reality) and US 11,062,523 (Creation Authoring Point Tool Utility to Recreate Equipment) — to build a mixed-reality layer that enables field technicians to inspect physical assets, log defects, and generate compliance evidence automatically.

The system integrates directly with the Aegis STARS schema — translating physical inspection data into regulatory compliance evidence packets without manual documentation. Assets inspected by TRIDENT automatically populate SSP narratives, quality management records, and audit trails.

Hardware development is underway with DE Design Works, primary hardware partner named in the NPS Evaluation Plan, with $200K allocated for prototype development. Target Q4 2026 milestone: lab-based validation of MR localization accuracy, digital twin fidelity, and hardware/software interfaces.
